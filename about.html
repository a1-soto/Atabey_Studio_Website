Meet Roger

Therapeutic massage specialist and Certified in Thai Massage. For him, massage is more than a technique, it’s an art form. His touch brings together precision, sensitivity, and creativity, turning each session into a multisensory experience that helps you reconnect with yourself.

Our Focus
